About Me

Hi, I'm Sara.

Certified personal trainer based in New Orleans, helping you build strength and confidence — one rep at a time.

My Story

I'm Sara, a New Orleans–based personal trainer who specializes in low-impact, accessible workouts that meet you where you are. After years of helping clients in person, I built my online coaching practice so that anyone — whether you're a senior looking for joint-friendly movement, a busy parent training at home, or someone new to fitness — can get expert guidance from your own home.

My approach blends low impact, Pilates-style strength work, dumbbell resistance training, mobility, and a motivational coaching style. No judgement, just accountability and being your biggest cheerleader — I love getting to know each of my clients on a personal level and customizing workouts that fit each individual's needs.

Credentials

  • Certified Personal Trainer (NASM-CPT)
  • Group Fitness Instructor Certified (NASM)
  • Senior Fitness & Low-Impact Training Specialist
  • Pilates Mat Instructor Certified
  • CPR & AED Certified — American Red Cross
  • Years of full-time, hands-on experience at local gyms and in-home training with clients
